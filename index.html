Reviews

Richard Seriton Winston II

Worth the Trip!

I'd like to preface this by saying that my family and I usually don't go on long trips, especially not for treats, but this was definitely worth the trip. It was like nothing that I've ever tasted before. How should I put this without being too vulgar? Well, the ice cream tasted like it came straight from a cow, an ice cream cow. And I was a calf slurping up the delicious ice cream from the teet. My first lick put me in a trance-like state, I must admit I was seeing a therapist before coming here, but the entire experience has healed me. I'm not the only one who would describe this experience like this, but my whole family did as well. We asked the owner whether he had drugged our ice cream, and he chuckled and told us, "No such thing was in there." he added, "That tasting such fresh ingredients for the first time can induce a trance-like state in some people." I definitely will be coming back, my family included!

Mariangela Herakles

Pleasantly Surprised.

Every few months or so, coming back from work, I usually see stores with signs saying "grand opening," Boy, could I care less. Just like clockwork, in they come, and out they go. I've created a game of sorts trying to predict how long it will take for these stores to close. And unfortunately, I've been stumped by this one, having changed my answer TEN TIMES. I mean, I'm no clairvoyant, but I have a keen eye for predicting the closures of these stores. But this one has been a nightmare, maybe I lost my spark, but then another store opened up, and I correctly predicted its closure, so my spark was still there. I mean, consider this, a store opens in winter which is really not that interesting, is it? But now consider that this is an ICE CREAM SHOP!!!! Every single day I see new people coming in and out. What kind of demented mind enjoys freezing to death? So, I theorized that this must be a money laundering business; surely their ice cream can't be that good. So, one day I decided to enter the shop, and boy, what a bore nothing special about it at all. But I must say that ice cream is pretty darn good. I usually hate being cold, but I didn’t mind it just this one time, take that as you will.

Hans Niemann
